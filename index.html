SANI AJI

I love to research

Sani Aji received the B.Sc. degree in mathematics from Gombe State University, Nigeria, in 2015, and the two master’s degrees in interdisciplinary mathematics and mathematical modeling from the University of L’Aquila, Italy, and the University of Silesia in Katowice, Poland, respectively. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the King Mongkut University of Technology Thonburi, Thailand. His research interests include iterative methods for solving system of nonlinear equations with applications to signal recovery, robotic motion control, image processing, machine learning, and computer vision.
